This module allows you to carry out warehouse movements in a faster and easier way.


Features

Barcode scanners

Use barcode scanners for every
inventory operation: inventories,
incoming shipments, packing orders, etc.

Internal Transfer

Manage the delivery and reception
of products as well as the consumption
of raw materials.

Purchase

Generate purchases by directly
associating the expense to the project
in question

Quick Info

Check the available quantity of products

Inventory Adjustments

Update the inventory of your products
while you go over your warehouse



For more information, visit the module website, that includes tutorials of the main operations.
